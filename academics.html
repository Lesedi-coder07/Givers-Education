How does it work

GE College offers both accredited Early Childhood Development Courses and Skills Development Facilitator courses.

Registration

Registration is done online through our website (www.giversfoundation.net) in which an application form can be completed online. If an applicant is accepted for registration, an invoice with bank details and student number will be sent together with final documents to be signed by an applicant. Registration will be finalised when proof of payment and signed documents are emailed and received by the institution. Thereafter an online meeting can be scheduled for induction.

Course fees

All fees are to be deposited to the institution’s bank account. Only after acceptance of the application has been confirmed, registration fee or total course fee may be deposited.

​Learning material and Devices

The institution provides each student with free electronic learning material. Each student that enrolls for distance learning must have basic computer literacy skills and access to a laptop/computer or smartphone with Microsoft Word through which to complete electronic assessments, submit assessments, participate in classroom chat groups and attend webinars. Material printouts are available for contact learners.

Distance Learning and Contact Learning

We offer both distance learning and contact learning sessions, so applicants must indicate during registration what mode of learning they prefer. Learning support will be provided to students through online webinar sessions and chat groups for distance learning students. Contact learning students will be facilitated in a classroom setup only at Pretoria Campus or at venue provided by the client organisation. Workshop/webinar dates and curriculum will be included in the programme handbook that will be sent together with learning material on or before induction date. The purpose of the webinar session is to prepare students for assessments and to give feedback on assessments. Distance learning students will receive all work electronically, and contact learning students will receive learning material hard copies .

Workplace/Practical assessments

It is the student’s responsibility to find relevant workplace from which he/she will complete workplace assessments if applicable. Evidence will have to be provided that a student was exposed to workplace.

Final assessment

Once all activities, assignments and assessments necessary to build a portfolio of evidence are submitted by student, an integrated assessment that demonstrates student’s ability to put theoretical knowledge to practice in a workplace must be submitted in form of pictures.
