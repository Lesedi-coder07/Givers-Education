Welcome to the admissions page of GE College. We're excited that you're considering joining our college.

Admission Requirements

To be eligible for admission, applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • High School Diploma or Equivalent
  • Satisfactory GPA
  • Standardized Test Scores (if applicable)
  • Letters of Recommendation
  • Personal Statement

Application Process

Follow these steps to apply to our university:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit the required documents, including transcripts and recommendation letters.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Wait for the admission decision.
