Application Process

Follow these steps to apply to GE College:



  1. Download Application Form Below (Scroll Down For Forms)
  2. All applications forms are submitted electronically to giverseducation@gmail.com and require a non-refundable application fee is R100 to be paid.
  3. If your application is accepted, you will receive an enrolment contract to complete,sign and return together with your registration fee deposit slip to the same email above. (See Fees Here)
  4. After receipt of your enrolment contract and proof of payment, you will receive an email invitation to attend an induction meeting..

Admission Requirements

To be eligible for admission, applicants must meet the following requirements:


  • ECD N4: Grade 9 or equivalent

  • ECD N5: Grade 10 or equivalent

  • ECD N6 (Certificate): Grade 11 or equivalent

  • ECD N6 (Diploma): ECD NQF level 5 National Diploma or National Senior Certificate (Matric)
