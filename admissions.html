Application Process

Follow these steps to apply to GE College:



  1. Download and carefully complete the comprehensive Application Form (Available Below)
  2. Submit your completed application form along with all required documentation to info@gecollege.co.za for our rigorous selection process.
  3. If your application meets our standards and you are selected from our applicant pool, you will receive an exclusive enrollment contract. This must be completed, signed and returned with your registration fee deposit slip to secure your place. (View Fee Structure)
  4. Following successful verification of your enrollment contract and payment, qualified candidates will receive an invitation to attend our mandatory induction meeting where we assess your readiness for our intensive academic programs.

Admission Requirements

To be eligible for admission, applicants must meet the following requirements:


  • ECD N4 (Certificate): Grade 11 or equivalent

  • ECD N5 (Diploma): National Senior Certificate (Matric)
